Dido (2021)

High-order ambisonic stage work. Commissioned by 212 Festival (Istanbul). Premiered at Bilkent International Laboratory Theatre, Ankara.

Dido explores spatialized sound, immersive listening environments, and the relationship between voice, memory, and political resonance.
